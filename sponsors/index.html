Sponsor National Day of Civic Hacking (NDoCH) SF

San Francisco is the global hub for technological innovation. Yet there exists serious tension between those who feel the benefits of that innovation and those who do not The time is ripe for an event to bring empathy, collaboration, and community-driven design to civic hacking.

We’ll be focusing our full attention on users — citizens, residents, community members — and diving deep to discover and define their problems to best understand how we can help. There will also be a range of skill-building activities taught by experts and designed to help civic hackers and their projects be effective and impactful.

We are proud to offer this opportunity to discover, network, and hack San Francisco. We hope you will help us reach our goals by supporting this global movement on a local level. Your sponsorship will allow us to host an accessible event for all ages and backgrounds.

Some key sponsorship benefits extend beyond the National Day of Civic Hacking event weekend. Don't see the amount or benefits you’d like? Get in touch with Fundraising [at] CodeForSanFrancisco.org
