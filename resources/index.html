Calling all passionate people who want to build skills to improve San Francisco. Attend SF’s National Day of Civic Hacking on Saturday, September 23 Code for America HQ, 155 9th Street, San Francisco! As part of a global event with thousands of people tackling local issues, this nontraditional hackathon will focus on improving the resiliency of San Francisco. In the face of disaster, are we prepared and ready for recovery? Come learn about efforts around individual, family and community resiliency happening now in San Francisco. You'll experience what resiliency means through a hands on scenario and leave knowing how you can contribute to a more resilient city.

We invite you to join us: get tickets to attend, join a workshop, sponsor the event, or partner with us.
