Calling all passionate people who want to build skills to improve San Francisco. Attend SF’s National Day of Civic Hacking, June 4 - 6 at Microsoft Reactor (downtown SF)! As part of a global event with thousands of people tackling local issues, this nontraditional hackathon will focus on developing impactful civic projects for the San Francisco Bay Area. All skills, interests, and people are welcome. Just be ready to learn, collaborate, and make a difference.

We invite you to join us: get tickets to attend, join a workshop, sponsor the event, or partner with us.
