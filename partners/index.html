Partner on National Day of Civic Hacking (NDoCH) SF

San Francisco has historically been known to welcome anyone and everyone into its Golden Gate. Yet that reputation is fading as The City becomes less affordable for some while a playground for others.

The time is ripe for Code for San Francisco to re-center its projects around positive, measurable impact for the people of San Francisco. We want to deliver, and NDoCH 2017 is all about bringing tech industry best practices to the communities most in need, whether they’re our dedicated civil servants, our expanding homeless population, or others.

SF's National Day of Civic Hacking is a chance for participants to build their skills in a variety of areas: human-centered design, open source software, community-driven design, user research, technical project scoping, and others. After the event, projects will continue their work and be incubated within the Code for San Francisco weekly hack night.

We want to partner with you to make a better San Francisco, using the National Day of Civic Hacking to kick things off. Below are different ways to support the event, starting a conversation together as partners.
